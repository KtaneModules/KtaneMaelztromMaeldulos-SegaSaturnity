On the Subject of the Ball Dial

Just how many sides does this ball have?

  • You must use the buttons to spin the ball until it displays a digit in the serial number, then press .
  • Use the table to determine the direction to spin the ball.
  • While the ball is displaying an arrow,
    treat the direction the arrow is pointed as oriented UP.
  • While the ball is displaying a word,
    use that word instead of the Previous Input Direction in the table below.
  • While the ball is displaying a number,
    press the next button that many times, ignoring anything displayed between.
    A zero may be treated as a one for quantity of button presses.
  • Begin by spinning the ball UP.
